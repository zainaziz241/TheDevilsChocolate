ABOUT

September 2019, during an intense class in HNC Marketing Communication, Azhar Rashad came up with the idea of creating his own business. ‘The Devil’s Chocolate’ which occurred to his genius mind.

The Devil’s Chocolate is a luxury brand, providing high quality luxury chocolates for all the chocolate lovers. This chocolate can be used in part-bags, it can be used for parties and mostly, it can be used to enjoy yourself with a cheeky treat!
